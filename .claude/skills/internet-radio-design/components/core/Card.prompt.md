Glass surface container. `panel` is the everyday blurred card; `sheet` is the heavier modal base with a deep shadow; `plain` is a flat translucent tile.

```jsx
<Card variant="panel" padding={24}>…</Card>
<Card variant="sheet">…modal contents…</Card>
```

- All variants use large soft radii and translucent fills — they need a dark background (and ideally the bg glow) behind them.
